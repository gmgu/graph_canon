3.9.1. degree_1.hpp¶

Full path: graph_canon/refine/degree_1.hpp

class refine_degree_1¶

Refine multiple degree 1-vertices connected to the same vertex.

Cells consisting entirely of degree 1 vertices, where all vertices have the same neighbour, with the same type of edge, will be split into trivial cells. Either Never or Unchanged will be returned and the appropriate automorphisms are reported.
