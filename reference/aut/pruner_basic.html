3.1.3. pruner_basic.hpp¶

Full path: graph_canon/aut/pruner_basic.hpp

class aut_pruner_basic : aut_pruner_base<aut_pruner_basic>¶

A Visitor for pruning the search tree based on automorphisms reported by other visitors. Stabilizers are computed conservatively by plain filtering of the generators.
